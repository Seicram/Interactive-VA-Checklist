System Compliance Checklist

📅 Date of claim

Ensure the Date of Claim is accurate in the system and matches the received documentation.

📄 End product (EP)

Verify the correct end product code is established for the claim type.

🏠 Address

Ensure the veteran’s address is up to date, including any temporary or alternate addresses.

🎖️ Active duty periods

Confirm all active duty periods are correctly entered in VBMS, including EOD, RAD, branch, character of service, separation reason, and verification status.

📝 Power of Attorney (POA)

Validate that POA information is accurate and accessible across all systems.
